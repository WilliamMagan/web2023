Purpose of the Page

The purpose of this page is to cover the various interests and phases of my life as I grew up. I have had multiple interest over the course of my life but a few of them have stuck around the first being fishing, which I have spent way too mush time and money on as whenever we get stationed near the ocean I would take off during the weekends to go fishing. The second interest I had was the military specifically the Army as thats what my father has done, so I made it my goal to join the army myself. The last interest that I have that stuck around was traveling which the Army has given us the ability to do, as the orders we would recieve would allow us to move around to other countries and experience those countries cultures and have new experience.
